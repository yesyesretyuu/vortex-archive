How Trading Works
1 Set a Trader ID above, then hit Publish My Collection so others can see your cards.
2 Go to Browse Players and search a friend's Trader ID to view their collection.
3 Click Trade with this player, pick a card you want from theirs, then pick one of yours to offer.
4 Hit Send Trade Offer — it lands in their Inbox.
5 They accept or decline. If accepted, cards swap automatically — you'll be notified via the Inbox badge.
